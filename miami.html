Miami

Miami a.k.a. the "Gateway to Latin America" is a city renowned for its deeply intertwined, multicultural infrastructure. With a multitude of outdoor, nightlife, and family friendly activities, Miami has rightfully earned its recognition as the 2nd most visited city in the United States, surpassed only by New York City.

City Insights

  • Population: 442,241
  • Year Incorporated: 1896
  • State region: Southeast
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average income: $28,772 (lower than the state average income: $55,681)
