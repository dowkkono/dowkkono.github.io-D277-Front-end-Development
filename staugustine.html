St. Augustine

St. Augustine was once one of two alternating capitals alongside Pensacola, essentially acting as a predecessor to the current capital, Tallahassee. This quaint city houses many historical landmarks with both Spanish and American influences.

City Insights

  • Population: 14,329
  • Year Incorporated: 1924
  • State region: Northeast
  • Classification: Urban and Rural
  • Average income: $32,521 (lower than the state average income: $55,681)
