Tallahassee

Tallahassee, the capital city of Florida, possesses a vibrant history ranging from the Civil War to the civil rights movement. Florida Agricultural and Mechanical University (FAMU) founded in 1887, serves as just one testament to the noble efforts of activists who fought tirelessly for equality in Florida and beyond.

City Insights

  • Year Incorporated: 1824
  • State region: Northwest
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average income: $31,152 (lower than the state average income: $55,681)
